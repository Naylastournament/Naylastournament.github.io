Archer Blood: Civilisation Saracens

The objective is to kill all your enemys units, and he will be defeated.
You spawn 20 archers every 40 seconds up to 160 archers. Kills will give you uppgrades on your units,
starting with 200 kills which give you crossbows. Chemistry with 400 kills and Arbalest with 600 kills.
Archer blood is a micro intensive map often played with many distractive units patrolled on no attack stance.


Khans Blood: Civilisation Mongols

The objective is to kill your enemies tower, and he will be defeated. Since kills give no advantages, rushing
towards an enemy and breaking their defenses immediately is the smartest way to play this. Khans are not able to
shoot at towers from outside an opponents base, so penetrating through enemy walls, gates or lines of Khans is
the only way to attack the tower. Khans is a simple map to understand, yet a map that will test your skills, speed,
reflexes and wits.


Radical Blood: Civilisation Japanese

The objective is to kill your enemies Accursed Tower, and he will be defeated.
The spawn island in the middle has two units at the start. You should take the throwing axemen, as they are the
best unit for 1v1. Move the throwing axemen onto the flag and the units will be created in your base. What you should
do next is take all of your axemen and send them to attack the enemys front towers. This is very important as getting
one raze fast is essential. The game from now on will rely on how you use these khans. If you can take out your
enemies khans then you can easily defeat him in battle. You will get free khans and sab reinforcements as
well! Use these units to go for kills. The last basic part of radical is pretty important. The panic. Use the king
to kill enemy units that are rushing your tower. Its best advised to use it against massed sabs.
You only get one so don't waste it.
